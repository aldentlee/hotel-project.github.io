The Azalea Garden Inn — Blowing Rock, North Carolina.

The Azalea Garden Inn, one of Blowing Rock's finest inn's, is known for it's beautiful landscaped grounds. From May 1 until mid-November, when the inn closes for the season, fragrant perennials and bushes cover the one acre setting.

Explore Blowing Rock, NC from our convenient location!

The Azalea Garden Inn is within walking distance of shopping, fine restaurants, art and craft exhibits, summer theater, and Blowing Rock Town Park Activities. In addition, there is easy access to the Blue Ridge Parkway; grandfather Mountain; several great golf courses; miles and miles of beautiful hiking and horseback riding trails; outstanding trout fishing; two national parks; and much more!

Stay in the Inn's Unique Log Cabin!

The Azalea Garden features a cozy log cabin. The outside structure is authentic, right down to its split rail fence, but, inside are all the plush comforts of the inn's other rooms, such as air conditioning and cable television. There is a romantic, log burning fireplace; a loft that overlooks the main room; a modern kitchen; full size bath; and a king size bed.

Cozy Guest Rooms

The rooms at the Azalea Garden Inn are oversized, custom finished, and individually decorated. Each room has either two full size or one king sized bed, free WIFI,and cable.

A little bit about us

The Azalea Garden Inn is owned and operated by Hank & Jimmie Knoll. After visiting the area during the fall season over 30 years ago, they fell in love with the Blue Ridge Mountains of North Carolina. The couple soon moved to Blowing Rock from South Holland, Illinois in 1979. Thus began the journey to create an Inn where you can relax and enjoy their beautiful gardens, streams, & decks. Visitors will also enjoy the comfort of home in the individually decorated rooms. We welcome you to stay and hope it is as memorable as possible!
